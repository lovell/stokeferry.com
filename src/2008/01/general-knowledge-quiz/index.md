General Knowledge Quiz

NICKNAMES

a. The Virgin Queen

b. The Merrie Monarch

c. Farmer George

a. The Holy City

b. The Eternal City

c. The bride of the sea

a. bobby

b. a tar

c. a clippie

a. The Land of the Midnight Sun

a. The First Gentleman of Europe.

b. The maid of Orleans.

c. The Little Corporal.
